...about

Hi there, I'm a software developer based in Sydney, Australia. Im originally from Houston, Texas and taught yoga for five years before deciding to move to Sydney, Australia in 2018. It was in Sydney where I discovered my passion for coding. After being awarded the Women in Tech Scholarship at Coder Academy, I was given the opportunity to dive deep into my passion of front and back end development.

In my spare time, Im either practicing my coding skills, teaching yoga classes, or volunteering.

...projects

Pocket Wellness App

My pocket Wellness App is a Ruby terminal application I built that customizes a yoga routine for office workers based on variables such as time, location and mental state. Office workers would be able to select whether they have as little time from 15 minutes to a whole break of an hour to practice different yoga stretches. This application was really fun to build, since it combined my passion of coding and yoga into one application.

My Portfolio Website

My Portfolio website was created using HTML and CSS. I wanted my website to be fun and a reflection of my personality while also displaying my experience and skills with the HTML and CSS tech stack.

Dog Match Marketplace

Dog Match is a rails application that matched dog shelters with people interested in pet adoption. My love of dogs inspired me to create a marketplace where people in NSW can search for a specific dog breeds and be matched to a local shelter that has their breed of choice.
